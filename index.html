Oh Hello!

My name is Ning Zhang, and I am currently a “Bad” PhD student in Economics and was a “Bad” Master’s student at department of Agricultural and Applied Economics in University of Arizona. I am preparing for Ph.D. programs and seeking opportunities to work with excellent scholars. At present, I am working as a Research Assistant with Dr. George Frisvold in the field of Environmental Resource Economics. Unfortunately, I am still uncertain about which specific areas interest me the most; therefore, I am actively reading and exploring various economic topics to find those that truly captivate me.

If you are interested in learning more about me, you can use the menu on this website to access additional information. I am confident that you will be pleasantly surprised by what you discover about me.

The most important thing is that I am always seeking opportunities to learn from excellent scholars.
